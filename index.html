Brief Resume

The project aims to develop a user-friendly website where users can review and compare cars, organized into categories such as model, design, engine, and power. It will include features like comments, a leaderboard for top reviews, providing accurate and real-time information. The website’s primary goal is to facilitate interaction between users and their cars, offering an inviting and accessible interface for users of all experience levels.
